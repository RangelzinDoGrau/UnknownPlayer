Evolution of the Dusty Depot in Fortnite (Chapter 1 Season 1 - Chapter 3 Season 3)

Dusty Depot was added to the map as an unnamed location.

October 12th 2017: Dusty Depot received it's name.

Dusty Depot, along with the factory, are the first removed locations to be added back to the map.

The Meteor hit the warehouses, completely destroying the blue Warehouse, and partially destroying the Grey Warehouse, renaming the are into Dusty Divot.

Inside the crater a government facility was erected, slowly being constructed throughout Season 4.

Trees started to grow in dusty divot throughout the season 4.

The remains of Dusty Depot have been changed, one part is now a tiny museum telling the story of the meteor that crashed down, destroying some of Dusty Depot and turned it into Dusty Divot.
